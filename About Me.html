About Me

Hello, I am Hans Ulit!

I loved cooking since I was a kid and now I want to share sharing my delicious recipes that I’ve learned throughout my life. Food has always been an important part of my Filipino culture and my family. Using this website, I want to create a community where everyone can share their recipes and discover new ones.

If your late for work and want something quick, special occasion where you need a lot of food recipes, or traditional dishes you want to taste again even your not home, you can find it all here.

What I want

My goal is to create a platform that brings together people from all walks of life to share their love for food. I want to build a space where people can learn new recipes, showcase their culinary creations, and engage with others who share the same passion. Join me in this journey and let’s cook together!
